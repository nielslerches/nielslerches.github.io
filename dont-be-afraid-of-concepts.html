Don't be afraid of (introducing) concepts

Thu 28 October 2021
By Niels Lerche Sørensen
Category: misc

During my last 3-4 years of my professional life I've sometimes had the need to introduce a new concept into an already existing design to help alleviate some constraint that proved too difficult to work with.

Sometimes you just need to break the rules.

That isn't to say that rules or constraints are completely bad. It's a guidance towards something that has already been atleast somewhat understood, and can help bring a team on the same page.
